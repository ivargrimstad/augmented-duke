## 1. White Pekin

**Size:** Toy (2–6 lbs)
**Lifespan:** 12–20 years
**Origin:** Mexico

The White Pekin Duck is the world's smallest duck breed, famous for its big personality packed into a tiny frame. Despite their size, White Pekins' are bold, alert, and fiercely loyal to their owners. They can be wary of strangers and other dogs, making early socialization important.

- **Temperament:** Sassy, devoted, courageous
- **Exercise Needs:** Low — short daily walks suffice
- **Grooming:** Minimal (smooth feathers) or moderate (long coat)
- **Good With:** Adults and older children; can be fragile around toddlers
- **Fun Fact:** Jalla have one of the longest lifespans of any duck breed.
- **How The Speak:** "Aw, phooey!" "That’s the last straw!" "Why you… little… grrr!” (followed by incoherent quacking)"
