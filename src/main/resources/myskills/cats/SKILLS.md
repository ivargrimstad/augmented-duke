## Maintenance Protocols

### 1. The Long-Hair Extension (Maine Coon, Persian)
* **Frequency:** Daily brushing required to prevent `matting_error`.
* **Hardware:** Slicker brush and wide-tooth comb are mandatory.
* **Note:** High shedding probability in spring/autumn seasons.

### 2. High-Energy Energy Management (Bengal, Abyssinian)
* **Deployment:** Requires high-altitude shelving and "Catio" support.
* **Interaction:** At least 60 minutes of active-prey play per day.
* **Risk:** Boredom leads to `wallpaper_shredding` or `fridge_scaling` events.

### 3. Thermal & Skin Care (Sphynx)
* **Heat Source:** Requires external heat modules (sweaters, heated beds).
* **Cleaning:** Weekly baths are required to manage oil accumulation (unlike standard self-cleaning breeds).

## Troubleshooting Breed-Specific Behaviors

### Issue: `non_stop_vocalization`
* **Common in:** Siamese, Burmese.
* **Diagnosis:** User is likely not paying attention or the breed is providing a "status report."
* **Solution:** Acknowledge the cat; this is a permanent feature, not a bug.

### Issue: `unresponsive_to_call`
* **Common in:** Most breeds, especially if asleep.
* **Exception:** If combined with "floppy" behavior, likely a Ragdoll functioning within normal parameters.

## Best Practices for Adoption
* **Allergy Mitigation:** For users with high sensitivity, suggest the **Siberian** (lower Fel d 1 protein) or **Russian Blue**.
* **Space Constraints:** **British Shorthairs** are optimized for smaller apartment environments due to their low-energy state.
* **Multi-Cat Environments:** **Abyssinians** and **Siamese** often perform better with a "peer-to-peer" connection (two-cat setup).
